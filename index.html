How to Play

Controls

  • Position: drag the striker along your base line.
  • Aim & Power: press anywhere else and drag backwards — slingshot style. The further you pull, the harder the shot.
  • Shoot: release. Press Esc while dragging to cancel.
  • Keyboard: ←/→ nudge striker, Space charge & release, P pause, F3 debug.

Objective

Pocket all nine of your coins. White plays white, Black plays black. The Queen (red) is worth 3 points but must be covered.

Rules implemented

  • Pocket one of your coins → you shoot again.
  • Pocket an opponent coin → it counts for them, your turn ends.
  • Queen cover: after pocketing the Queen you must pocket one of your own coins on the same or the very next shot. Fail → the Queen returns to the centre.
  • Foul (striker pocketed, or your last coin pocketed while the Queen is still on the board): one of your pocketed coins returns to the centre and your turn ends. If you have none pocketed, you owe one (debt) and pay it on your first pocket.
  • Pocket all your coins with the Queen resolved → you win. Timer runs out → turn passes. Turn cap reached → draw.

Physics

Coins are rigid discs with mass, momentum and restitution. Collisions are resolved with true impulse maths, friction decelerates coins linearly (independent of mass, as with real sliding friction), and everything is integrated at a fixed 240 Hz sub-step so fast strikers never tunnel through coins.
